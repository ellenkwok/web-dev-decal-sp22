About Me

Hi! I am Ellen.

I am a fourth year undergraduate student at UC Berkeley studying Cognitive Science and minoring in Data Science. I am passionate about social equity, education, and language accessibility.

I have experience in UX Research, Data Analysis, and Design. Currently, I am an undergraduate teaching assistant for the course, Introduction to Data Science, at UC Berkeley. I also worked as an UX researcher in the non-profit, Pandemic Professors, and as an implementation specialist at Insellerate.
